## Description

Decido Now is built for one job: helping you stop hovering and start moving.

Most apps give you long plans, endless lists, or a chat that never ends.
Decido Now is different.

You tell Decido Now what you want, what is blocking you, how much time you have, and what kind of energy you are working with.
Decido Now gives you one smart next move first, not a cluttered dashboard.
Then it explains:

- why this move fits you
- what it gives you today
- how to start
- what gets easier tomorrow

Then you act.
Then you score the outcome.

Over time, Decido Now builds your Decision DNA:

- when you move best
- which goals create momentum for you
- what kind of blockers slow you down
- what format helps you follow through

Core features:

- No-scroll command screen with one main move
- Goal + blocker based decision flow
- Guidance layer with why it fits, what you gain today, how to start, and what to do tomorrow
- Learn, Language, Earn, Focus, Reset, Health, and Social tracks
- Decision Outcome Score
- History and streak tracking
- Decision DNA insights
- Smart Start onboarding with a first visible result
- Free live signals for learning, language, and money context
- Shareable move output

Decido Now is designed for people who do not need more inspiration.
They need one smart move they can trust.

## Review Notes

- No account is required.
- Core decision data is stored locally on device.
- Live cards use public data sources with caching.
- AI coaching is optional and can fall back to local summaries.
- Auto-renewable subscriptions are configured as `decido.pro.monthly` and `decido.pro.yearly`.
- The main experience is available without notifications.
