# Decido Now Review Notes

Decido Now is a standalone decision and action app.

## Basic review flow

1. Open the app.
2. On the Today tab, the app shows one main move based on your current context.
3. Tap `Open guidance` to view why it fits, what you gain today, how to start, and the next step for tomorrow.
4. Tap `Swap move` if you want a cleaner alternative, or `Refine` to adjust the current setup.
5. Tap `Share` to generate a shareable move summary.
6. Tap `Start this move` to log the action.
7. The move appears in Progress.
8. Open the move and add an outcome score.
9. Visit Progress to view DNA pattern cards and the weekly life report after enough scored moves.

## Notes for App Review

- No login is required.
- Notifications are not required.
- Decision history is stored locally on device.
- Public informational cards may appear for finance and learning content.
- AI coaching is optional and can fall back to local summaries when no AI service is configured.
- The onboarding flow is designed to lead the user to one first visible result rather than a dashboard.
- Auto-renewable subscriptions are configured as `decido.pro.monthly` and `decido.pro.yearly`.
- If the reviewer is using a build environment where the store is unavailable, the purchase UI gracefully falls back without blocking the core app flow.
