# Decido Launch Plan

## Soft Launch Goals

- Validate that users understand the product in under 30 seconds
- Measure whether users complete at least one decision on day 1
- Measure whether users return to score outcomes
- Validate whether Learn / Language / Earn categories feel valuable

## Week 1 Targets

- 100 to 300 installs
- 30%+ of users complete at least one decision
- 15%+ of users return the next day
- clear feedback on repetitive suggestions and confusing copy

## Traffic Sources

- short-form video showing one real decision flow
- X/Twitter demo clips
- Product Hunt style positioning
- founder-led posts with before/after examples

## Core Messages

- Stop overthinking fast
- Get 3 clear choices
- Pick one and act
- Learn your Decision DNA

## First Monetization Goal

- Do not optimize for maximum price first
- optimize for strong Pro value and clear conversion
- use Monthly and Yearly only

## First Post-Launch Priorities

- improve weak suggestion areas
- refine onboarding
- strengthen premium value
- test better screenshots and icon variants
