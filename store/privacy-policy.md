# Decido Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 30, 2026

## Overview

Decido is a decision support app designed to help users choose one small action quickly, track whether they completed it, and view patterns from their own decision history.

## What data the app stores

The app stores the following information locally on the device:

- decision preferences such as goal, blocker, mode, energy, time, budget, and category
- generated decision options
- the option selected by the user
- completion status and result score
- short written reflections entered by the user
- chosen app language
- local premium preview state used during development
- cached live cards such as finance and learning summaries

## Where data is stored

Decision history, preferences, and cached live cards are currently stored locally on the device using app storage.

## Public data sources

The app may request public information from third-party sources to display live cards, including:

- foreign exchange data
- crypto price snapshots
- public knowledge summaries

These requests are used to show in-app informational cards and do not require account creation.

## Optional AI coaching

If optional AI coaching is enabled by the developer, a limited subset of recent decision records may be sent to a developer-controlled backend in order to generate a short coaching summary.

This may include:

- recent selected categories
- goal and blocker fields
- completion state
- result score
- short reflection text

AI coaching is optional and the app can fall back to local summaries when no AI service is configured.

## What data is not collected by default

Decido does not currently require account creation.
Decido does not currently collect email, name, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location by default.
Decido does not currently sell user data to third parties.

## Payments

If in-app purchases are enabled in a future release, payments will be processed through Apple In-App Purchase. Decido itself will not store full payment card details.

## Analytics and diagnostics

The current build does not include third-party analytics SDKs. If analytics or crash reporting are added later, this policy should be updated before release.

## User controls

Users can delete the app to remove locally stored data from the device. If cloud sync or accounts are added in a future version, in-app deletion controls will also be added.

## Children

Decido is not designed for children under 13.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as features change. Material updates should be reflected in the App Store privacy details and inside the app.
