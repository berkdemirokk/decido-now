# Decido TestFlight Copy

## What to Test

Decido helps you stop overthinking and make one clear move in seconds.

Please test:

- creating a decision from the Today tab
- selecting one of the 3 choices
- scoring the result in History
- checking Decision DNA after multiple reviews
- reading Smart Start, Mandatory Move, finance, and learning cards

## What to Send Back

- confusing or weak suggestions
- any crashes or freezes
- moments where the app feels repetitive
- anything that makes the review flow unclear
- feedback on whether the app feels useful enough to open daily
